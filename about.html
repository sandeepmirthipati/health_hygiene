Why Health Matters

Healthy living starts with everyday choices

HealthWise helps students, families, and local communities understand daily habits that protect energy, dignity, learning, and long-term wellbeing.

Importance of Hygiene

Hygiene is everyday prevention

Personal hygiene protects the body, preserves dignity, and reduces the spread of common infections. Clean surroundings support mental comfort as much as physical health.

Healthy BodyClean skin, teeth, hair, and nails reduce avoidable illness.
Healthy MindClean routines and restorative sleep improve mood and attention.
Healthy EnvironmentWaste control and safe water protect whole communities.
Preventive HealthcareVaccination, early help, and daily habits prevent bigger problems.

WHO Healthy Living Principles

A practical framework for everyday wellbeing

Clean

Wash hands, care for your body, and keep shared spaces safe.

Nourish

Eat varied foods, drink safe water, and limit excess salt, sugar, and ultra-processed food.

Move

Build activity into daily life through walking, sport, chores, stretching, and play.

Recover

Protect sleep, mental health, social connection, and time away from screens.

Prevent

Use vaccination, mosquito prevention, safe food, first aid awareness, and timely medical help.
